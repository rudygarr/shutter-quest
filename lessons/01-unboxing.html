Canon R7 — Know Your Camera

Objective

Identify all R7 parts, insert battery/SD card, attach strap, power on, and take your first 10 photos.

What You'll Learn

  • Every button, dial, and port on the R7
  • Battery insertion (it only goes one way!)
  • SD card slot location (it's behind the battery door)
  • Strap attachment (don't skip this — cameras bounce)
  • Power switch location
  • Taking your first photos without overthinking
🎯 Challenge

Take 10 photos of anything. Just click. Don't worry about settings, composition, or "getting it right." Point the camera at things, press the shutter, and hear that sweet mechanical sound. The goal is comfort, not perfection.
