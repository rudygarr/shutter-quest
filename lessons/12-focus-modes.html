Focus Modes — One Shot vs Servo vs AI

Phase 3: Focus Like a Pro 🤖 Together Coder (Llama 3.3 70B) 150 XP

Objective

Select the correct AF mode for every subject. One Shot for still, AI Servo for motion, AI Focus for "I don't know what this is doing."

🎯

One Shot AF

  • Locks focus on half-press
  • Stays locked until release
  • Beep confirmation
  • Focus → recompose OK

Use for: Portraits, landscapes, still life, posed groups

🏃

AI Servo AF

  • Continuously tracks focus
  • Predicts subject movement
  • No beep (continuous)
  • Focus priority = tracking

Use for: Sports, kids running, pets, wildlife, any motion

🤷

AI Focus AF

  • Starts as One Shot
  • Switches to Servo if subject moves
  • Camera decides
  • Can hunt in low light

Use for: "I have no idea what this subject will do" — unpredictable kids, candid events

R7-Specific Focus Power

  • Dual Pixel AF II: Every pixel = focus pixel = fast, accurate
  • Eye AF: Detects human/animal eyes in ALL modes
  • Subject detection: People, animals, vehicles
  • Touch & Drag AF: Tap LCD to pick focus point, drag to move
  • Servo characteristics: Adjustable tracking sensitivity, acceleration/deceleration

When to Use What — Quick Decision

  • Subject perfectly still? → One Shot
  • Subject moving predictably? → AI Servo
  • Subject unpredictable / "maybe": → AI Focus
  • You're unsure / chaotic event: → AI Servo + Eye AF (safest)

R7 Pro Tip: Servo Customization

Menu → AF → Servo AF characteristics:

  • Tracking sensitivity: Locked (sticky) ↔ Responsive (jumps fast)
  • Accel/decel tracking: How fast it reacts to speed changes
  • Default: Good for 90%. Tune for specific sports.
🎯 Challenge

Portrait (One Shot), running kid (Servo), confusion test. Shoot: (1) Posed portrait with One Shot + Focus/Recompose, (2) Kid running across frame with AI Servo + Eye AF, (3) Unpredictable toddler with AI Focus. Note which mode nailed it.
