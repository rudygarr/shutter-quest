Portfolio & Pitch — THE FINAL BOSS

Phase 6: Event Photographer Mode 👑 CEO (Nemotron 3 Ultra) 500 XP

Objective

Build a portfolio that gets you hired. Craft a pitch that closes the deal. This is your graduation — and your business card.

The Portfolio Formula (15 Images Max)

🎯 5 Hero Shots

  • Best portrait
  • Best action/sports
  • Best event moment
  • Best creative/artistic
  • Best detail/macro

📚 5 Sequence Stories

  • 3-shot event sequence
  • 3-shot portrait story
  • 3-shot process/detail
  • 3-shot emotion arc
  • 3-shot environment

🎯 3 Specialty Showcases

  • Flash mastery (fill/bounce/HSS)
  • Light mastery (golden hour, flash, mixed)
  • Focus mastery (stacking, tracking, stacking)

🧠 2 Behind-the-Scenes

  • Setup shot (lighting diagram + result)
  • Workflow shot (cull → edit → deliver)

Each Image Must Have

  • Caption: What, Where, When, Settings (f/2.8, 1/250, ISO 400)
  • Story: 1 sentence — what was happening, why it matters
  • Settings visible: EXIF intact, settings listed
  • Category tag: Portrait / Event / Sports / Creative / Detail

The Pitch Deck — 5 Slides, 3 Minutes

Slide 1: WHO

Your name, specialty (Event/Portrait/Sports), location, years exp, "I capture moments that matter."

Slide 2: WHAT

Services: Event coverage (4hr/8hr), Portraits (individual/group), Sports (team/individual), Details (products/venue). Packages start at $XXX.

Slide 3: WHY YOU

3 differentiators: (1) Dual-camera workflow = zero missed moments, (2) Same-day sneak peeks, (3) 48hr full gallery delivery. Testimonials.

Slide 4: PROOF

3 best images per category. 15 images total. Each with 1-sentence story. QR code to full gallery.

Slide 5: NEXT STEPS

CTA: "Book a 15-min call / Reply to this email / Text 555-XXXX." Availability calendar link. Thank you.

Delivering the Pitch

  • Email: Subject: "Photography for [Event/Org] — 3 min pitch inside"
  • PDF: 5 slides, mobile-friendly, < 5MB
  • Link: Gallery (password: event+date) + Calendly link
  • Follow up: 48hr → 1 week → 1 month (3 touches max)

The Live Pitch (Final Exam)

Schedule 15-min Zoom with teacher/mentor. Present 5-slide deck. Answer: "Why you?" "What if light fails?" "What's your backup plan?" Walk away with: feedback, confidence, maybe a real gig.

🏆 FINAL CHALLENGE

Build your portfolio (15 images + captions + settings). Build your pitch deck (5 slides). Record a 3-min Loom presenting it. Submit all three. Reward: 500 XP + "Shutter Quest Graduate" badge + Portfolio review + Real pitch opportunity if you want it.
