Focus Areas — Point, Zone, Wide, Face/Eye

Phase 3: Focus Like a Pro 🤖 Together Coder (Llama 3.3 70B) 150 XP

Objective

Select the right AF area for your subject. Point for precision, Zone for groups, Wide for chaos, Face/Eye for people/animals.

🎯

1-Point AF

  • Single focus point
  • You place it exactly
  • Maximum precision
  • Focus → recompose works

Use for: Portraits (eye), macro, still life, precise control

🎯+

Expand AF Area

  • 1-point + 4/8 neighbors
  • Helps if subject moves slightly
  • Still precise-ish

Use for: Portraits with slight movement, confident but safe

⬜

Zone AF

  • Named zones (9, 15, etc.)
  • Camera picks within zone
  • Flexible Zone = resize/move zone

Use for: Groups, action with predictable path, sports

⬛

Wide Area AF

  • Full frame coverage (100% x 100%)
  • Camera picks ANYWHERE
  • Subject detection takes over

Use for: Chaos, unpredictable action, "I don't know where they'll be"

👁️

Face/Eye Detection AF

  • Detects faces → prioritizes eyes
  • Human + Animal (dog/cat/bird) + Vehicle
  • Works in ALL focus modes
  • Eye priority: Nearest eye / Left / Right

Use for: Portraits, events, pets, wildlife, weddings — ALWAYS ON for people

🔄

Flexible Zone AF (R7)

  • Resize zone: 3x3, 5x5, 7x7, etc.
  • Position zone anywhere in frame
  • Combine with Servo for tracking

Use for: Sports (zone on goal), dance (zone on stage), custom coverage

R7 Specific: AF Area + Mode Combos

One Shot + 1-Point

Static precision. Portrait, macro, landscape.

AI Servo + Eye AF

Moving people/animals. Best for events, sports, pets.

AI Servo + Zone

Predictable action. Sports, dance, known path.

AI Servo + Wide

Chaos mode. Unpredictable, crowds, wildlife.

R7 Flexible Zone AF — Custom Shapes

Menu → AF → AF area → Flexible Zone → Choose size (3x3 to 15x15) → Position with joystick. Save as custom C1/C2/C3 modes.

Pro setups:

  • Basketball: 15x15 zone at hoop height
  • Soccer: 9x9 zone at goal line
  • Dance: 7x7 zone center stage
  • Church aisle: 5x15 vertical zone

Focus + Recompose — When It Works & Fails

  • Works: f/4+, moderate distance, One Shot, center point sharpest
  • Fails: f/1.4, close subject, wide angle, AF-S with Servo
  • Why: Focus plane is flat; recompose rotates plane → subject falls out of focus
  • R7 fix: Use Eye AF + Touch & Drag AF instead of recompose
🎯 Challenge

Portrait (Eye AF), group (Zone), action (Wide), chaos (Wide + Servo). Shoot: (1) Portrait with Eye AF + 1-Point, (2) Group of 4 with Zone, (3) Running kid with Servo + Zone, (4) Chaotic playground with Wide + Servo.
