Event Workflow — Gear, Settings, Backup

Phase 6: Event Photographer Mode 🤖 Together Coder (Llama 3.3 70B) 200 XP

Objective

Build a bulletproof event workflow. Gear prep, settings dialed, backup strategy, and crisis management. Zero surprises on game day.

The Pro Gear Kit (What's in the Bag)

📸 Bodies

  • Primary: R7 (or R6/R5)
  • Backup: R8 / RP / 2nd R7
  • Same battery (LP-E6NH)

🔍 Lenses (The Trinity)

  • Wide: 16-35mm f/2.8 or 24-70mm f/2.8
  • Normal: 50mm f/1.8 or 85mm f/1.8
  • Tele: 70-200mm f/2.8 or 135mm f/1.8

⚡ Power

  • 4x LP-E6NH batteries
  • 2x USB-C PD power banks
  • Dual charger (charge 2 at once)
  • In-camera charging (USB-C PD)

💾 Storage

  • 4x CFexpress Type B (512GB/1TB)
  • 2x SD UHS-II (backup)
  • Card wallet (organized)
  • USB-C card reader (CFAST/CFe)

⚡ Flash & Light

  • Speedlite: 600EX II-RT or EL-1
  • Diffuser dome + bounce card
  • AA batteries (Eneloop Pro x8)
  • Radio trigger (ST-E3-RT)

🎒 Essentials

  • Dual camera strap (BlackRapid/Peak Design)
  • Rain covers (camera + flash)
  • Microfiber cloths x4
  • Lens pens + rocket blower
  • Gaffer tape + zip ties
  • Business cards + contract

Pre-Event Settings Baseline (Set Night Before)

  • Mode: Av (Aperture Priority)
  • Auto ISO: 100-6400 (Max), Min Shutter: 1/250
  • Focus: AI Servo + Eye AF + Zone AF
  • Drive: High Speed Continuous (15fps mech / 30fps electronic)
  • WB: Custom Kelvin (start 5500K) or AWB
  • File: RAW (C-RAW) + JPEG (slot 2)
  • Dual Card: RAW to CFexpress, JPEG to SD
  • Flash: ETTL, FEC -1, HSS ready
  • Custom Modes: C1=Event, C2=Portrait, C3=Sports

Battery & Card Management During Event

  • Battery swap at 30%: Never wait for red
  • Rotate batteries: 4 batteries → 2 in charger, 2 in cameras
  • Card swap at 50%: Never fill a card completely
  • Dual slot = instant backup: Never format until home + verified
  • Cull in-camera: Rate 1-5 stars during downtime

Crisis Playbook

  • Flash dies: Bounce off wall, or wide + high ISO + wide aperture
  • Battery dies: Swap body (that's why you have 2)
  • Card full/error: Swap card (that's why you have 4)
  • AF hunting: Switch to 1-point + center, or MF + peaking
  • Flash recycle slow: Lower FEC, raise ISO, wider aperture
  • Lens error: Clean contacts, swap lens (that's why you have 3)
🎯 Challenge

Pack your bag for a 4-hour event. Lay out everything. Photograph the packed bag. Explain each item in 1 sentence. Time yourself — under 10 minutes? Unpack, repack, time again.
