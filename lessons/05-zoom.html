Zoom With Your Feet

Phase 1: Hold This Thing 🤖 Together Coder (Llama 3.3 70B) 100 XP

Objective

Understand focal length, compression vs expansion, and why moving your feet beats zooming the lens.

What You'll Learn

  • Focal length = field of view + magnification
  • Wide (24mm) = environmental, expansion
  • Normal (50mm) = human eye perspective
  • Telephoto (200mm) = compression, isolation
  • Crop factor on R7 (1.6x) = 24mm = 38mm equivalent
  • Why "zoom with feet" changes perspective, zooming doesn't

🔭 R7 Focal Length Cheat Sheet (1.6x crop)

24mm (38mm eq)
Wide environmental
35mm (56mm eq)

Sports/action

The "Zoom With Feet" Rule

Zooming = crops the image, same perspective

Moving = changes perspective, relationship between subject and background

Get closer with wide = subject big, background environmental

Step back with telephoto = subject same size, background compressed

🎯 Challenge

Same subject at 24mm, 50mm, 105mm, 200mm. Move your feet to keep subject same size in frame. Compare: background relationship, subject distortion, compression. Upload all 4.
