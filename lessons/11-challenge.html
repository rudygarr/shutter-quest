Exposure Triangle Challenge — Boss Battle

Phase 2: Exposure Triangle 👑 CEO (Nemotron 3 Ultra) 300 XP

Objective

Prove you own the Exposure Triangle. Recreate 3 iconic looks using ONLY Manual mode. No Auto, no Av, no Tv. Just you and the big M.

The Three Missions

Each mission gives you a LOOK. You figure out the settings. Shoot, review, adjust, nail it.

🎯 Mission 1: "Golden Hour Portrait"

The Look: Subject separated from dreamy background, warm golden light, tack-sharp eyes.

Target: f/1.8–f/2.8 • 1/250–1/500 • ISO 100–400

Hints: Wide aperture for separation. Fast enough shutter for micro-movements. Low ISO for clean skin. Watch for backlight!

50 XP

🎯 Mission 2: "Landscape Layer Cake"

The Look: Foreground rock sharp, mid-ground trees sharp, background mountains sharp. Deep depth of field.

Target: f/11–f/16 • 1/125+ • ISO 100

Hints: Narrow aperture for depth. Tripod helps. Focus 1/3 into scene. Watch shutter if wind moves grass.

50 XP

🎯 Mission 3: "Frozen Splash"

The Look: Water droplet frozen mid-air, crisp edges, no motion blur. Frozen moment invisible to eye.

Target: 1/1000+ • f/4–f/5.6 • ISO 400–800

Hints: Fast shutter freezes water. Aperture wide enough for light, narrow enough for droplet sharpness. Burst mode + timing.

50 XP

🎯 Mission 4: "Night Street Panning"

The Look: Car sharp, background light-streaked. Motion frozen on subject, environment blurred with color streaks.

Target: 1/30 • f/8 • ISO 100 • Night

Hints: Panning technique. AI Servo + Zone AF. Follow through. 1/30 = Goldilocks for 30-40mph.

50 XP

🎯 Mission 5: "High Key Dream"

The Look: Bright, airy, slightly overexposed on purpose. Soft, dreamy, "Instagram aesthetic."

Target: f/2.8 • 1/250 • ISO 200 • +1.5 EV

Hints: Deliberate overexposure. Wide aperture for softness. +1.5 EV on purpose. White dress/background works best.

50 XP

🎯 Mission 5: "Silhouette Sunset"

The Look: Subject pure black, sky burning with color. Shape tells story, no detail needed.

Target: f/8 • 1/500+ • ISO 100 • -2 EV

Hints: Expose for SKY, not subject. Spot meter off sky. -2 EV locks subject black. Shape must be recognizable.

50 XP

🏆 Boss Battle Rules

  • Mode dial: M only — no Av, Tv, P, Auto
  • No auto ISO — you set it
  • No exposure compensation — you own the meter
  • Each mission: Shoot → Review 100% → Adjust → Reshoot until perfect
  • Upload final 6 images (1 per mission) with EXIF intact
  • Write 1-sentence "cheat code" for each mission for future you
👑 Final Boss: The 6-Mission Gauntlet

Complete all 6 missions in ONE shooting session. No breaks longer than 5 min. Upload all 6 with cheat codes. Reward: 300 XP + "Exposure Triangle Master" badge + Unlock Manual-only missions.
