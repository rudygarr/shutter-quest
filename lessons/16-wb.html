Custom Kelvin — Your Secret Weapon

  • Menu → White Balance → K (Kelvin)
  • Dial exact temperature: 2500K–10000K
  • Lower K = warmer (orange)
  • Higher K = cooler (blue)
  • Gym lighting? Dial 3500K. Golden hour? Dial 6000K.
  • Saves you from fixing every photo in post

Custom WB (Gray Card) — The Pro Way

  1. Buy 18% gray card ($10) or use white paper in pinch
  2. Place card in SAME light as subject
  3. Fill frame with card
  4. Menu → Custom WB → Select image → OK
  5. Set WB to Custom (icon: two triangles + square)

Pro tip: Do this EVERY time light changes. Takes 15 seconds. Saves hours in post.

WB Shift/BKT — Fine Tune

  • Menu → WB Shift/BKT
  • Move dot on grid: A (amber) ← → B (blue), G (green) ← → M (magenta)
  • Subtle creative shifts: +A2/M1 = warm skin tones
  • BKT = bracket 3 shots with different shifts
