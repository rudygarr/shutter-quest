Exposure Compensation — The Cheat Code

Phase 2: Exposure Triangle 🤖 Together Coder (Llama 3.3 70B) 150 XP

Objective

Master the +/- button. Override the meter instantly without leaving Av/Tv modes. The fastest way to fix exposure.

What You'll Learn

  • EV (Exposure Value) = 1 stop = double/half the light
  • +/- button (or dial) = shift meter target
  • +EV = brighter image (more exposure)
  • -EV = darker image (less exposure)
  • Works in Av, Tv, P modes — NOT in Manual (use ISO/aperture/shutter instead)
  • R7: Av dial on back, or Quick Control dial

🎚️ EV Scale — Your Cheat Sheet

-3
Very dark
-2
Dark subject
-1
Backlit
0
Meter says OK
+1
Snow/beach
+2
Bright snow
+3
Blinding

When to Use What

🎯 Common Scenarios

☀️ Snow / Beach
+1 to +2 EV

Meter sees bright → underexposes

🌅 Backlit Subject
+1 to +2 EV

Meter reads background, not face

🌑 Black Cat / Coal
-1 to -2 EV

Meter sees dark → overexposes

🌅 Sunset Silhouette
-2 EV

Subject dark, sky colorful

🏟️ Bright Stage
-1 EV

Bright lights, dark surroundings

📸 High Key Portrait
+1 EV

Bright, airy "blogger" look

How It Works (Av/Tv/P Modes)

  • Av mode: +EV → slower shutter; -EV → faster shutter
  • Tv mode: +EV → wider aperture; -EV → narrower aperture
  • P mode: Camera shifts both
  • Manual mode: EC does NOTHING (you own all 3)

R7 Quick Access

  • Back dial: Turn while holding +/- button (or just dial in newer firmware)
  • Quick Control: Tap screen → tap +/- → dial
  • Scale: Visible on LCD/EVF bottom
  • Reset: Press +/- button once (or dial to 0)
