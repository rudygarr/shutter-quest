Flash — The Pocket Sun

Phase 4: Color & Light ✍️ Creative (MiniMax/Qwen) 150 XP

Objective

Master on-camera flash: fill flash for harsh sun, bounce flash for soft light, and when NOT to use flash.

What You'll Learn

  • Fill flash: balance harsh shadows in bright sun
  • Bounce flash: ceiling/wall = soft, directional light
  • TTL vs Manual: let camera decide vs you control power
  • Flash exposure compensation (FEC): +/- on flash power
  • High-speed sync (HSS): flash at 1/8000s for wide-open apertures in sun
  • Diffusers/bounce cards: soften the punch
⚡

Fill Flash

  • Bright sun → harsh shadows
  • Flash lifts shadows
  • TTL auto-balances
  • FEC: -1 to -2 for subtle

Use for: Outdoor portraits, harsh noon, backlit subjects

🏠

Bounce Flash

  • Point at ceiling/wall
  • Large soft source
  • Natural direction
  • Ceiling = 8-10ft ideal

Use for: Indoor events, receptions, low ceilings

⚡

High-Speed Sync

  • Flash at 1/8000s
  • f/1.8 in bright sun
  • Less power (pulsed)
  • HSS icon on flash

Use for: Wide-open portraits in sun, shallow DOF + flash

🚫

When NOT to Flash

  • Beyond range (built-in ~12ft)
  • Ambient is beautiful already
  • Flash would ruin mood
  • Museums/no-flash zones

Rule: If it looks good without, don't add flash

Fill Flash — The Most Useful Technique

  • Mode: Av or P, Flash: ETTL (TTL)
  • Flash Exp Comp (FEC): -1 to -2 stops for subtle fill
  • Subject in sun, shadows on face → add fill
  • Dial FEC down until shadows lift but don't disappear

🎯 Bounce Flash Technique

1
Point flash head UP (90°) at white ceiling
2
If ceiling high/dark: bounce off side wall
3
Add bounce card (pull out white card) for catchlights
4
Flash power: TTL auto, or Manual 1/4-1/16 power

High-Speed Sync (HSS) — f/1.8 in Bright Sun

  • Normal sync: 1/250s max (R7: 1/250s)
  • HSS: pulses flash at 1/8000s (less power)
  • Enable on flash: HSS button (lightning + H)
  • Trade-off: less power = closer range or higher ISO
  • Use: f/1.8 portraits at noon with fill flash
🎯 Challenge

Fill flash, bounce flash, no-flash comparison. Same subject, same sun: (1) No flash, (2) Fill flash -1 FEC, (3) Bounce off ceiling, (4) HSS at f/1.8. Compare: shadow lift, natural look, catchlights.
