Manual Mode — The Big Leagues

Phase 2: Exposure Triangle 👑 CEO (Nemotron 3 Ultra) 200 XP

Objective

Full manual control. You balance all three exposure variables. The meter is your guide, not your boss.

🎯 The Exposure Triangle

Change one → adjust another to keep meter at 0

What You'll Learn

  • Mode dial to M — you own all three variables
  • Light meter: 0 = "correct" exposure, + = brighter, - = darker
  • Sunny 16 rule: sunny day = f/16, 1/ISO shutter
  • Workflow: set aperture for look → set shutter for motion → adjust ISO for meter
  • Exposure compensation in Manual = shifts meter target

Manual Workflow (Every Shot)

  1. Aperture first: What depth of field do I want? (f/1.8 portrait, f/11 landscape)
  2. Shutter second: What motion look? (1/500 freeze, 1/30 blur, 1/125 safe)
  3. ISO third: Adjust until meter hits 0
  4. Review: Check histogram, highlight alert, adjust

Sunny 16 — Your Mental Cheat Code

Sunny day: f/16, shutter = 1/ISO (ISO 100 = 1/100s)

Slight overcast: f/11

Overcast: f/8

Heavy overcast/open shade: f/5.6

Sunset: f/4

When Meter Lies

  • Snow/sand: Meter sees bright → underexposes → +1 to +2 EV
  • Black cat/coal: Meter sees dark → overexposes → -1 to -2 EV
  • Backlit subject: Meter reads background → subject dark → +1 to +2 EV
  • Spot meter: Meter off subject's face, lock, recompose
🎯 Challenge

5 manual shots in different light. Sunny, shade, backlit, indoor, mixed. Use Sunny 16 as starting point. Adjust from there. No Auto modes allowed.
