Leading Lines, Frames & Layers — Composition Power Tools

Phase 5: Composition & Story ✍️ Creative (MiniMax/Qwen) 150 XP

Objective

Master three composition power tools: leading lines that guide the eye, frames that isolate, and layers that create depth.

Leading Lines — The Eye's Highway

  • Lines guide viewer's eye TO your subject
  • Types: straight (roads, rails), curved (rivers, paths), diagonal (dynamic), converging (perspective)
  • Start at edge/corner, lead TO subject
  • Wide angle (24mm) exaggerates lines beautifully

Frames Within Frames — Isolation & Context

  • Doorways, windows, arches, trees, hands, mirrors
  • Frame isolates subject + adds context/location
  • Shoot THROUGH something to frame subject
  • Frame doesn't need to be complete — suggestion works

Layers: Foreground, Middle, Background

  • 3 layers = instant depth
  • Foreground: something close (fence, flowers, person's shoulder)
  • Middle: your subject
  • Background: context, environment, sky
  • Aperture f/2.8-f/4: subject sharp, layers separated but visible
  • f/11+: everything sharp, layers compressed

📏 Leading Lines

  • Roads, rails, fences, rails
  • Shadows as lines
  • Architectural lines
  • Natural: rivers, ridges, waves

🖼️ Frames

  • Doorways, windows
  • Tree branches, arches
  • Mirrors, reflections
  • People's arms/hands

📚 Layers

  • FG: grass, railing, person
  • MG: your subject
  • BG: building, sky, crowd
  • Aperture = layer separation

🎯 Combining Them

  • Line leads THROUGH frame
  • Frame within frame within frame
  • FG line leads to MG subject in BG frame
  • Layers + lines = 3D feel
🎯 Challenge

3 photos: leading lines, frame within frame, 3 layers. (1) Strong leading line to subject (road, rails, shadow), (2) Subject framed by doorway/window/tree, (3) Clear FG/MG/BG layers with f/4 aperture. Upload all 3.
